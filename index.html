Gather your family and friends to celebrate the summer solstice for a day of fun at Majorvilles Woodward Farm to celebrate the 150th anniversary of the founding of Majorville!


Our theme this year is lumberjacks. Our town traces its history back to the early days of lumbering in Michigan.




Quick Facts

  • The event will take place Thursday, June 20, 2024 from 11am-10pm.
  • This is th Summer Solstice Festival of Majorille Michigan.
  • The event will take place at the Woodward Farm in Majorville.
  • This event is being held by the Chamber of Commerce in Majorville, Michigan
